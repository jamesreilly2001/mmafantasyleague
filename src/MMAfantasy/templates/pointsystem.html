Our points system rewards the users knowledge of mma and how much risk they're willing to take. Users have got to take a fighter's potential fight value into account versus their opponent. Our algorithm takes the fighters previous 5 fight results, the fighters takedown accuracy and the fighters strike accuracy into account to generate their potential points for a win. From the last five of each fighter's fights, 1 point is added to their potential score for each loss. The algorithm also adds points where relevant to the fighter that has a worse overall strike and takedown accuracy. This method can really display who is the favourite fghter for the fight, as the lower the points rewarded, the more in favour the fighter is to win.

Will you reap the rewards of picking an underdog, or will you play it safe and choose the favourite?
